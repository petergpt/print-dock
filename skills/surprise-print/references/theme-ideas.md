# Theme Ideas

Use these directional tweaks when the user asks for a specific vibe.

## Playful Editorial (Default)

- Add to prompt: "optimistic palette, whimsical collage shapes, joyful motion, polished magazine look"
- Good for: upbeat daily news syntheses, science and culture mixes, surprise gifts

## Retro Documentary

- Add to prompt: "archival film-grain texture, muted palette, editorial realism"
- Good for: historical events, political moments, early inventions

## Dreamy Scientific

- Add to prompt: "luminous atmosphere, precise instruments, optimistic futurism"
- Good for: astronomy, expeditions, engineering milestones

## Bold Poster

- Add to prompt: "strong silhouettes, geometric composition, high-contrast blocks"
- Good for: sports, transportation, dramatic public events

## Quiet Human Moment

- Add to prompt: "intimate scene, soft light, candid body language"
- Good for: biographies, cultural firsts, social history
